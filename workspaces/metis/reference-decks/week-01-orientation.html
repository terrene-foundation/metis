Week 1

Key Concepts You Need

Training vs. Inference

Training = learning from data.
Inference = making predictions.
Training is expensive; inference is cheap.

Overfitting

When a model memorizes the training data instead of learning patterns. Performs great on old data, fails on new data.

Bias-Variance Trade-off

Simple models miss patterns (high bias). Complex models memorize noise (high variance). The art is finding the balance.

Feature Engineering

Creating new data inputs that help the model learn better. Often more important than choosing the right model.
